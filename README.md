# Sportswear Retail Performance Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analysing sales, profitability, product performance, seasonality, and discount effectiveness in a sportswear retail context.

## Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard Preview](images/dashboard-preview.png)

## Key Features

- Year-to-date sales, profit, units sold, and operating margin
- Previous-year comparison with conditional formatting
- Interactive filters for date, region, category, and store type
- Monthly sales and profit trend
- Top 10 products by sales
- Discount impact on sales and profit

## Key Insights

- Sales are broadly stable year to date
- Profit has improved slightly
- A small group of products drives a large share of revenue
- Full-price and lightly discounted products generate most sales and profit
- Higher discounts do not appear to improve performance in this dataset

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data modelling
- Time intelligence
- Conditional formatting
